AccountStatus

Indicate the status of the account. All accounts are created in the "INACTIVE" state.

NameNumberDescription
ACCOUNT_STATUS_UNSPECIFIED 0

ACCOUNT_STATUS_INACTIVE 1

Initial state after creation. Once moved from INACTIVE to anything else, cannot come back.
ACCOUNT_STATUS_ACTIVE 2

Normal operations can proceed.
